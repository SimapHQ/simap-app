New Item

Every piece of food or household item that you store is represented as an item. You can choose which category to group an item under, define different units for each item, and specify volume requirements for adults and children.

Item Information

Item Units

Define the different units you'll use so that the tracker can do all your math for you. Depending on the item, you'll have different units. For flour, you have define lbs and cups, but for canned vegetables you might use oz and '16oz cans'.

1 is equivalent to master units.
